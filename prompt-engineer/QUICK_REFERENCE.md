## If Things Go Wrong

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| **Low HN engagement** | Double down on Twitter/Reddit (they'll work). Resubmit HN next day with tweaked title. |
| **Negative comment** | Respond thoughtfully, explain your choices, ask for feedback. Be genuine. |

| **Slow GitHub growth** | That's okay Day 1. By day 3-7, growth will accelerate as more people discover it. |
| **Low Reddit votes initially** | Comments matter more than upvotes early on. Engage deeply with comments. |
